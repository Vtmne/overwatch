In the beginning

I was born in Selma, California on July 17th, 1986. I lived in Selma until the 8th grade, that was when I moved to Fresno and spent the rest of my life as well as a year between the ages of 12 and 13 in Los Angeles. I graduated from Sunnyside High Scool in 2005.

Military Life

I enlisted right out of High School at the age of 17in 2004.I turned 18 while in boot camp and didn't tell anyone unlike Pvt Jackson who thought it would be a good idea to let the Drill Sargeant know it was his birthday, he got smoked all day. After boot camp I arrived at the beautiful Fort Huachuca, Arizona and began Interrogator training. I deployed to Afghanistan from 2007-2008 in support of OEF VIII.
